What is PicoCTF?
> PicoCTF is an online Capture The Flag (CTF) competition designed primarily for high school and college students to learn and practice cybersecurity skills. It was created by the security experts at Carnegie Mellon University's (CMU) Institute for Software Research (ISR). The competition provides a fun and educational way for participants to dive into various aspects of computer security, including cryptography, web security, forensics, binary exploitation, and more.
